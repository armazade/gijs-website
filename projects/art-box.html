Art Box

Product Design

Project Info

  • Type: Product Design
  • Client: Art Salvage

Themes

  • Art
  • Restoration
  • Safety

Currently most packaging and transport of artworks is done very traditionally, by creating an exclusive wooden crate for each painting. Art Salvage gave the assignment to design an art transport box suitable for both paintings and 3D models.

The main objective was to make the box suitable for housing paintings of different sizes and shapes. It should be fully equipped to withstand external influences—temperature changes, fluctuating humidity levels, and vibrations—without damaging the artwork contained inside.
